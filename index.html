✦ About USD Acre

What is USD Acre?

USD Acre is a yield-bearing stablecoin that helps your capital grow while staying pegged to the U.S. dollar. It combines stable-value utility with automated yield generation, allowing individuals, treasuries, and platforms to earn without adding operational complexity.

✦ How It Works

Simple for users. Powerful under the hood.

AcreFi is built to make yield-bearing stable assets easy to use while preserving the flexibility needed for advanced treasury and platform use cases.

01

Deposit or mint USD Acre

Users or businesses acquire USD Acre as a stable-value asset designed for programmable finance and passive capital productivity.

02

Capital is allocated to yield strategies

BloomFi deploys capital into carefully selected DeFi opportunities with a focus on efficiency, risk controls, and accessibility.

03

Yield accrues automatically

Users benefit from native value accrual without manually moving assets between protocols or managing multiple yield positions.

04

Redeem or integrate anytime

Whether you are an individual holder, a treasury team, or a platform, AcreFi keeps funds accessible and integration-ready.
